Viewing the approximate average stock per choice

The following metrics further define metrics added in TG JE v2023.2. Previously, metrics showed Approx Stock Units By Site (S/T% and Min Pres), which are site totals across all choices. These new metrics utilize an additional divisor for TY Act New Choices, to provide averages per choice:

  • Avg Approx Stock Units By Choice By Site (S/T %): Approx Stock Units (S/T%) / TY Plan Site Count / TY Act New Choices

  • Avg Approx Stock Units By Choice By Site (Min Pres): Approx Stock Units (Min Pres) / TY Plan Site Count / TY Act New Choices
